# Regularity versus Naturalness: The Wahl–Jespersen Discussion on an International Language (1935)

A rare record of a direct meeting between the creators of Novial and Occidental — the two leading figures of the naturalistic school of interlinguistics — in which each author sets out his design principles in his own language. Jespersen and de Wahl held this discussion in Helsingør in 1935.

- Original text: [Discussion pri International Lingue](http://interlanguages.net/OJEW.html) / [PDF](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Discussiones_inter_E._de_Wahl_e_O._Jespersen.pdf)
- Translation and notes: [7shi/wahl_jespersen](https://github.com/7shi/wahl_jespersen)
