# Wahl Jespersen

Translation of "DISCUSSION PRI INTERNATIONAL LINGUE".

This document records a discussion held in Helsingør, Denmark in April–May 1935 between Otto Jespersen (creator of Novial) and Edgar de Wahl (creator of Occidental, later renamed Interlingue), two leading figures of the naturalistic school of interlinguistics. Published in the June 1935 special issue of the Novial journal *Novialiste*, it compares the design principles of their respective languages and reflects the state of the international auxiliary language movement at its most active period. Each author wrote in his own language, making the document itself a demonstration of both Novial and Occidental.
